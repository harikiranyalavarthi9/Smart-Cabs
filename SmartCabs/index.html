about SmartCabs

SmartCabs has been specifically designed keeping in mind the requirements of a cab operator.

A cab operator provides vehicles on hire to customers and bills them according to the tariff card.

A cab operator operates in a service industry, which requires high standards of efficiency to be successful.

A cab operator has to keep tight control over these factors to be competitive and remain profitable.

The key success factors in this business are quality of service, condition of the vehicle, competitive pricing, customer-interface etc.
